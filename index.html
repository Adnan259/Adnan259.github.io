About Me

Building Logic,
Signal by Signal

I'm a fresh Electrical & Electronic Engineering graduate from Gopalganj Science and Technology University, now working as a Trainee RTL Design & Verification Engineer at ADN Semiconductor in Mohakhali, Dhaka.

My focus is semiconductor front-end design: writing clean, reusable RTL in SystemVerilog, building class-based verification environments, and debugging waveform-first. On the SC-SoC project — a single-core RV32IMF RISC-V SoC — I've designed peripheral modules and found and fixed real RTL bugs in FIFO, CDC, and UART blocks.

I work comfortably across AMBA (AXI4, AXI4-Lite, APB), WISHBONE, and OBI interconnects, and I document everything I design and verify.
